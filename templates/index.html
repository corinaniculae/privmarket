PrivMarket

PrivMarket is a proof of concept that aims to introduce a new data flow for implementing a user data market.

Its design goals of privacy and anonymity are further analysed within, providing a insight on a new paradigm of designing location aware systems. PrivMarket shifts the point of control from the service providers, that acted as data aggregators so far, back into the network, leveraging the revenue model over the users as well.
